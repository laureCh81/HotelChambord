Hôtel & SPA CHAMBORD

The promise of an incomparable hotel experience.

A manor house for some, a castle for others, the Hotel Chambord is apprehended as a place apart which welcomes epicureans, history buffs, lovers of Paris. The rooms and suites, each key given by the butler, have the allure of a pied-à-terre. Every hallway seems lead, as in the past, to ceremonial apartments

The rooms

Designed for modern travelers in search of absolute comfort, our rooms are among the most spacious rooms in Paris and are equipped with state-of-the-art technology. From the classic room to the suite, enjoy an incomparable quality of service.

Discover our rooms

The SPA

The Chambord Spa, the largest luxury spa in the capital with its 1800m², is a real invitation to well-being, serenity and refinement. Here, the art of French beauty and the millennial expertise of oriental medicine are perfectly combined.

Discover our SPA and treatments

The Restaurant

In keeping with the French art of living, the window at 1er rue Montaigne houses a restaurant. From breakfast to dinner, including tea time, it offers light and gourmet meals. Soups and salads but also a choice of hot dishes. To be enjoyed indoors, or on the heated terrace, with a breathtaking view of the Eiffel Tower.
