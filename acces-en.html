How to get to the hotel

En train

The Hotel is located between 10 and 20 minutes from all Parisian train stations. However, if you prefer the metro, take line 9 - Alma Marceau station or the RER C station Pont de l'Alma.

We also have the possibility to book a car with driver, do not hesitate to contact the concierge.

By plane

Possibility of car with driver : reservation and information at the concierge: concierge@hotelchambord.com

Shuttle bus – Shuttle "Le Bus Direct" (ligne 4) will take you to Montparnasse train station, which is a 12-minute taxi ride from the hotel.

Métro / RER – If you don't have a lot of luggage, the RER B is an excellent alternative. Change to RER C at Saint-Michel station, get off at Pont de l'Alma. The Hotel Chambord is then a 4-minute walk away, crossing the bridge.
